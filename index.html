How We Built OpenScout: Tracking Brand Mentions Across Social Media

Every brand gets talked about online, on Reddit threads, Twitter conversations, LinkedIn posts, and Hacker News discussions. The problem is finding those mentions before they go cold.

That's why we built OpenScout. It's a SaaS tool that automatically monitors Reddit, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Hacker News for relevant brand mentions, scores them using AI, and suggests ready-to-post replies.

How it works

You submit your brand's website URL. Our AI analyzes it, recommends relevant subreddits and keywords to track, and starts crawling. Every post and comment is scored on three dimensions: brand relevance, buying intent, and engagement opportunity. You only see the conversations that actually matter.

The reply suggestions are platform native. Reddit replies sound like Reddit. LinkedIn replies are professional. Hacker News replies are technical and direct. No generic, templated responses.

Who it's for

OpenScout is built for SaaS founders, marketing teams, and growth marketers who want to engage in real conversations where people are already discussing problems their product solves. Instead of cold outreach, you show up where the demand already exists.

If you're interested in social listening that goes beyond vanity metrics and actually helps you find customers, check out OpenScout.
